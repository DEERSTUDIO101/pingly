What you get

Built for people,
not advertisers.

Every decision in Pingly was made with one question: does this protect the user?

🔒

End-to-End Encryption

Every message, call, and file is encrypted before it leaves your device. Not even Pingly can read your messages.

📵

Zero Ads. Ever.

We don't sell your data or show you ads. Pingly is free because we believe privacy shouldn't cost money.

📞

Encrypted Video Calls

HD video and voice calls with the same encryption as your messages. Nobody is watching but you two.

👥

Group Chats

Create groups, invite friends by username, and keep the conversation private — even in groups of hundreds.

⏱

Disappearing Messages

Set messages to vanish after a time you choose. From one hour to one week — your call.

🔑

Device Key Generation

Your encryption keys are generated on your device and never leave it. True zero-knowledge architecture.

How it works

Up in 60 seconds.

STEP 01

Download the app

Available for Android and iOS, completely free. No credit card, no subscription, no catch.

STEP 02

Create your account

Pick a username and password. Your encryption keys are generated on-device during sign up — nobody else has them.

STEP 03

Add friends by username

No phone number required. Find friends by their username and send a request — privacy from day one.

STEP 04

Start chatting

Every message is encrypted end-to-end. Send text, images, files, and make calls — all private by default.
